We Are Feane

Founded in 2010 by chef Marco Reyes and his wife Elena, Feane started as a humble corner diner with ten tables and a menu of five dishes. The dream was simple: serve extraordinary food quickly, without ever compromising on quality or warmth.

Over fifteen years, that dream has grown into one of the most beloved fast-casual restaurants in the city — but our philosophy hasn't changed. Every morning we source fresh ingredients from local farms. Every plate is prepared with care and every guest is welcomed like family.

Today, Feane serves thousands of guests every week across multiple locations, yet we still treat every meal as if it's the first one we ever made.

Our Journey

Every great story has a beginning. Here are the key moments that shaped who we are today.

  • 2010
    The First Diner

    Marco and Elena open Feane's first location — a cozy 10-table diner in downtown New York with five signature dishes.

  • 2013
    Online Ordering Launch

    We introduced our online ordering platform, making Feane's flavours accessible to the whole city.

  • 2017
    Second & Third Locations

    Rapid growth led us to open two more branches, expanding our team to over 80 passionate food lovers.

  • 2023
    Sustainability Pledge

    We committed to 100% compostable packaging and partnered with local urban farms for zero food waste.

  • 2025
    Today & Beyond

    Serving 50,000+ happy guests monthly across 4 locations — and still growing with the same original passion.
